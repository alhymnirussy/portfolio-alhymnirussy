Airport Taxi Sharing (Android App)

An Android-based mobile application designed to enhance the efficiency of airport taxi services by enabling drivers to input the passenger’s drop-off location from the airport and define a surrounding radius. Passengers within this radius who intend to travel to the airport can view and book the same taxi for their return trip. This approach enables taxis to avoid empty return journeys, optimizes fuel consumption, and improves driver efficiency.
